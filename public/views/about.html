Hi! My name is Steven Lim, a software engineer who loves to explore. This blog is a detailed discription of my travels around the world, food I eat, and a reminder to always seek out adventure.

"Life begins at the end of your comfort zone."

If you know me personally, you know that I can be pretty introverted, reserved, and sometimes (okay maybe all the time) awkward. One of the greatest things about traveling is that it forces you out of your comfort zone. I think that is why I am so enamored with exploring and traveling because it pushes me to try new things and enjoy different experiences.

I hope that through my stories you get motivated to try new things and break away from the norm, regardless of where your travels take you. If you want to keep following along or want to get into contact with me, feel free to click the links below. Happy travels!

- Steven
